Mood Music Study

We are a team at the University of Bristol running a research study to explore the relationship between music and mood.
What is the research about?

We would like to understand how the way we listen to music is related to our mood. To do that, we need to measure mood and music listening over time. We're looking for participants to help!

What will participants do?

People who sign up to the study will need to do some initial questionnaires and agree for us to collect their Spotify listening data. Then they will need to answer five questions about their mood 5 times a day for 2 weeks. At the end, we'll ask everyone to complete some final questionnaires, and send their listening data to us.

Who can take part?

You can take part if...
You are a University of Bristol student who is over 18
You have a Spotify Premium account
