How to request your personal data from Spotify

Spotify allows you to download your personal user data from the 'Privacy Settings' section of your Spotify account. Here is how to request it:

  1. Go to www.spotify.com/us/account/privacy/. You might need to log in.
  2. Go to the bottom of the page to a section called 'Download My Data'.
  3. If you follow the instructions then Spotify will email you a link to download you data. This usually happens within 24 hours.
  4. Once you have recieved your data, come back here and use the box below to upload it.

Upload your listening data

You will have had several files sent to you. The ones you need to upload will be called StreamingHistory.json. These contain a record of all the songs you have listened to. There might be several of these files which are numbered, please upload them all.
